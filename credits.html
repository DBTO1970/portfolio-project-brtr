In trade, barter is a system of exchange in which participants in a transaction directly exchange goods or services for other goods or services without using a medium of exchange, such as money.

Why | BRTR | ?

Centuries-old method of commerce, based on the trade of goods or services without relying on money.

Find artisans, crafters and producers of goods and services in your location, and support a robust and fair method of trade.

Maximize your productivity, by trading a good or service locally, with someone else in need.

Find other like-minded people locally, while supporting a larger community.

Goods and Services

If it can be made, grown, fixed, built, or consulted on, you can likely find someone willing to barter it.

Local availability of goods and services is limited by geographic location. However, many goods and services can be exchanged via shipping or digitally!

With a growing network of | BRTR | members, more and more goods and services can be traded and exchanged!

Community

Join other creative and talented individuals, as we make this world a little better, a littler cleaner, a little more fair.

Securely trade using the | BRTR | platform as a way to communicate and safely arrange for trades in local 'safe zones'. With real-time messaging, your personal information never needs to be shared to arrange trades! Just message and arrange terms, time/method/location of trade, and you are all set!
