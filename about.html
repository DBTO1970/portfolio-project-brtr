Why | BRTR | ?

Centuries-old method of commerce, based on the trade of goods or services without relying on money.

| BRTR | serves to connect craftspeople, artists and makers of all sorts so that they may trade their goods or services with each other. | BRTR | is not a "web store", nor are there any goods for sale. If you would like to relate | BRTR | to something, then consider it a social connection platform geared toward those who make goods and/or provide services they are willing to trade or barter.

Members are able to search and connect with each other, and arrange any transactions in the 'real world' in person or via shipping.

Members may provide website or contact information at their own discretion, and may opt to sell goods outside of this platform.
